1   About me

I am a Graduate Assistant / PhD Student in linguistics at the University of Lausanne. I hold a Bachelor in History and Linguistics from the University of Lausanne as well as a Master of Languages in General Linguistics from the University of Uppsala. I have previously been a Research Assistant in history on the INIDUN project, and I have a previous background in IT and communication.

2   Research
